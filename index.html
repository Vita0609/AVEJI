How we work

Algorithm for working with us for convenience and understanding of the project

Idea

The client comes to us with an idea. It can be an image/sketch or another reference. And we think about how to implement it, make technical drawings and offer solutions for materials.

Technical task

The client comes to us with an idea. It can be an image/sketch or another reference. And we think about how to implement it, make technical drawings and offer solutions for materials.

Commercial offer

We prefer to use natural materials. But we can optimize any estimate by simplifying materials or design. We can adjust to the client's budget and agree on a commercial offer.

We will create working documentation and drawings. This is the foundation of high-quality production. At this stage, we approve all dimensions and materials, drawings with the client and begin production.
